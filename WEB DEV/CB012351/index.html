Located in stunning area of Seraya Hill in Batam, King's Hotel Batam situated stategically in the Northern area of Batam.
It is only 5 minutes away from Nagoya Shopping Mall, 15 minutes from Harbour Bay Ferry terminal, and 30 minutes from Hang Nadim International Airport.





The four-star hotel provides 104 guestrooms and meeting rooms, offering guests and travelller’s alike fantastic views from the top, with a dazzling scenery of Singapore. King’s Hotel Batam offers a magnificent and the best view in Batam.
